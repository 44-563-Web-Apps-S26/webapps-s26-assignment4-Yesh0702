Yeshwanth Light Opera

The Mikado

Act II, Scene: Ko-Ko's Garden

YUM-YUM: How can I look my best when I am so miserable?
PITTI-SING: There, there, don't cry.
PEEP-BO: You'll have plenty of time for that later.
YUM-YUM: But to be buried alive!
PITTI-SING: It is a very disagreeable situation.
(Enter Ko-Ko, looking very much dejected)
KO-KO: There you are, my poor child.
YUM-YUM: Oh, Ko-Ko, is there no hope?
