Yeshwanth Light Opera

The Mikado

Act I, Scene: A courtyard in Titipu

PISH-TUSH: And I'm the one to do it! (Stage direction: he bows low)
KO-KO: Gentlemen, I am much touched by this reception.
POOH-BAH: It is the least we can do for a man of your distinction.
KO-KO: I am a man of the people, after all.
PISH-TUSH: A very remarkable man, nonetheless!
POOH-BAH: (Aside) He doesn't know the half of it.
KO-KO: Let us proceed to the festivities!
